OpenCV Zoo SFace model attribution

Source:
https://github.com/opencv/opencv_zoo/tree/main/models/face_recognition_sface

Model:
face_recognition_sface_2021dec.onnx converted to Core ML as FaceEmbeddingModel.mlmodel.

OpenCV Zoo states that all files in models/face_recognition_sface are licensed
under the Apache License 2.0.

The model is a MobileFaceNet instance trained with SFace loss and contributed to
OpenCV Zoo by Yaoyao Zhong.
